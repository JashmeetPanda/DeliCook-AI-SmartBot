French Fries

Preparation time: 15 minutes + freezing

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 5-6 Milly Potatoes, peeled
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp Paprika powder
  • ¼ cup Parmesan Cheese

Process

  1. Cut the potatoes in ½ cm thickness and put them in water for 15 minutes.
  2. In a pot, add water and keep it to simmering temperature, i.e., 80-85°C, and par-boil them.
  3. Once done, drain them in a flat tray and spread evenly.
  4. Deep freeze them for 1-1 ½ hour.
  5. Heat oil in a kadhai at 130°C for the first frying.
  6. Remove them from the freezer and fry them for about 80%.
  7. Once fried, remove them on a paper towel.
  8. Heat oil in a kadhai at 170°C for the second frying.
  9. Fry them completely. Remove them on a paper towel.
  10. Transfer hot fries in a mixing bowl, sprinkle some salt, and mix well.
  11. Sprinkle paprika powder and garnish with parmesan cheese.
  12. Serve hot.

Note:

  • Milly potatoes have excess starch content, which makes them crispy.
  • Leaving the potatoes in the water releases the starch content.
  • Parboiling them at a simmering temperature cooks them evenly. Parboil them about 20%.
  • Freezing them after boiling makes them crispy and prevents them from turning black.
  • Double frying makes them crispier.
